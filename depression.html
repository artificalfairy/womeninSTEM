Depression

What is Depression?

Depression is a common and serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, the way you think and how you act. Depression causes feelings of sadness and/or a loss of interest in activities you once enjoyed. Women in STEM fields are more likely to report higher levels of stress and anxiety and higher incidences of depression.
